# Playtesting: The Spider-like Arakene (Non-Human Heroes) in Swords of the Serpentine

**By Kevin Kulp**

## Overview

The Arakene are predatory creatures who worship Llolgoth, a vast spiritual spider that has absorbed corrupted divine essence. They infiltrate human society, particularly the city of Eversink, to manipulate events and advance their schemes. Their true forms are "pale translucent skin covered with coarse black bristles" featuring eight eyes and fluid-draining mouths, though they maintain glamours to appear human.

## Ancestry Abilities

Arakene characters gain four core abilities:

- **Ancestry (Arakene)**: Grants knowledge of Arakene culture and language; provides a glamour making the character appear human
- **Corruption**: Reflects ties to sorcerous power
- **Scurrilous Rumors**: Represents skill at gathering secrets through networks of informants
- **Spirit Sight**: Enables detection of deific energy and deceased gods

## Character Creation Guidance

The article recommends high Stealth ranks (8+) for espionage-focused characters and suggests selecting spider or web-related sorcerous spheres when taking Corruption ranks. Players may sacrifice bonus investigative points to gain Prophecy or Skulduggery for enhanced information gathering.

## Sample Characters

**Cobril Delverlane** exemplifies an Arakene: a Transmutation professor balancing genuine academic work with manipulation schemes. Stats include Sorcery 8, Sway 8, and diverse investigative abilities reflecting both scholarly expertise and conspiratorial influence.

The **Arakene Lie-Master** adversary demonstrates how these creatures function as antagonists, embedding themselves within communities to create manipulated social networks.

## Designer's Intent

The mechanics capture themes from drow elves, Lovecraftian fiction, and infiltration narratives while maintaining distinctions from traditional fantasy races.
