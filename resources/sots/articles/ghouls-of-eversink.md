# The Ghouls of Eversink: Gluttonous Undead for Swords of the Serpentine

**Author:** Kevin Kulp

## Overview

The article presents three ghoul stat blocks designed for the Swords of the Serpentine RPG system. These creatures inhabit the sub-basements of Eversink, a sinking city, representing themes of gluttony, greed, and corruption.

## Design Philosophy

A key mechanic involves ghouls draining victims' Athletics pools while inflicting Health damage. When Athletics reaches zero, victims become paralyzed. This design choice creates utility for Refresh tokens, allowing players to recover from paralysis by spending one and raising Athletics to 1.

## Common Ghouls

**Description:** Rotting humanoid forms with jagged teeth and clawed fingernails that hunt from ceilings and shadows.

**Combat Stats:**
- **Health Threshold** 4, **Health** 12
- **Morale Threshold** 4, **Grit** 3 (reduced to 0 by faith symbols)
- **Warfare** +1; **Damage Modifier** +1

**Special Abilities:**
- Paralysis (drains 1d6+1 Athletics per successful attack)
- Spider Climb
- Lightning Speed (costs 3 Malus; grants +1 Health/Morale Thresholds)
- **Stealth Modifier** +2

Defeated corpses may transform into new ghouls unless fully devoured.

## Ghoul Rats

**Description:** Animated rat corpses operating in swarms, targeting single victims sequentially.

**Combat Stats:**
- **Health Threshold** 1, **Health** 1
- **Morale Threshold** 3, **Grit** 3
- **Warfare** +0; **Fixed Damage** 3

**Abilities:** Paralysis (drains flat 3 Athletics), Spider Climb

## Ghoul Lord

**Description:** Intelligent leaders of ghoul hives, typically too bulky for active hunting but accumulating secrets from consumed victims.

**Combat Stats:**
- **Health Threshold** 4, **Armor** 3
- **Health** 5 per Hero
- **Morale Threshold** 4, **Grit** 6
- **Warfare** +2; **Damage Modifier** +2

**Special Abilities:**
- Extra Action (costs 3 Malus; usable twice per round)
- Fear (automatic Morale damage without rolling)
- Paralysis (drains 1d6+3 Athletics)

Ghoul lords can shed their flesh to escape as common ghouls over approximately one hour.
