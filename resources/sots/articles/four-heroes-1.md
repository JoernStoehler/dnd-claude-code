# Four Heroes: Your Swords of the Serpentine Source for Pre-Made Heroes

**Author:** Kevin Kulp

## Overview

Kevin Kulp introduces a series showcasing sample heroes for *Swords of the Serpentine*, demonstrating character creation possibilities. These examples often subvert traditional sword-and-sorcery tropes and are typically rooted in Eversink, the game's primary setting.

---

## Sister Claris - Inquisitor of Denari (Sentinel)

**Personality:** Cynical, stubborn, plain, righteous, proud

**Drives:** Addressing the wicked; changing the world; exalting friends

**Combat Stats:**
- Health Threshold: 3-6 | Armor: 2 | Health: 8
- Morale Threshold: 4 | Grit: 1 | Morale: 10
- Sway: 10 (Damage +1 from guilt)
- Warfare: 5 (Damage +1 from flail)

**Key Abilities:** Command 1, Intimidation 1, Taunt 2, Trustworthy 1, Felonious Intent 2, Laws & Traditions 1, Spirit Sight 1, Vigilance 1

**Gear:** Round shield, tea collection, badge, letter of authority, coin armor, grandmother's flail, prayer book, dormitory keys, painting with adventuring friends, holy coin

**Design Notes:** Claris serves as a church sentinel investigating sorcerous corruption. She excels at defense and social interaction while being difficult to charm.

---

## Exorius of the Inner Eye - Sorcerer

**Personality:** Pretentious, amused, spoiled, covetous

**Drives:** Revealing true power; making others indebted; being essential to important events

**Combat Stats:**
- Health Threshold: 3 | Armor: 1 | Health: 8
- Morale Threshold: 4 | Grit: 1 | Morale: 10
- Sorcery: 10 vs. Health (Damage +1 from aging)
- Sway: 3 (Damage +1 from bombastic style)

**Key Abilities:** Command 1, Intimidation 1, Corruption 5, Forgotten Lore 1, Prophecy 2

**Sorcerous Spheres:** Aging, Art, Decay/Entropy, Mirrors, Transportation

**Gear:** Shimmering robes, pouch connecting to alternate timeline selves, spinning mirrors, throne, imprisoned enemies in paintings, key from mother's letter

**Design Notes:** Inspired by Leiber's Ningauble character, Exorius is a spectacularly powerful sorcerer who ensures his relevance. His gear creatively explains his mechanical abilities.

---

## Vincenzo - Town Crier (Thief)

**Personality:** Friendly, inquisitive, helpful, polite, honest

**Drives:** Staying alive; spreading truth; protecting friends

**Combat Stats:**
- Health Threshold: 3 | Armor: 0 | Health: 8
- Morale Threshold: 4 | Grit: 1 | Morale: 10
- Sway: 12 (Damage +1 from convincing)
- Warfare: 1 (unarmed)

**Key Abilities:** Charm 1, Command 2, Servility 2, Trustworthy 1, City's Secrets 1, Scurrilous Rumors 3

**Gear:** Threadbare clothing, daily story list, route through The Tangle, coin purse, royalist claimants, treason invitation, birthmark, hovel, trusted friends

**Design Notes:** Unconventional thief lacking burglary and stealth. Instead, Vincenzo possesses extensive city knowledge and persuasive ability, complicated by technical claims to Eversink's crown (abandoned five generations prior).

---

## Foyle - Professional Monster Hunter (Warrior)

**Personality:** Pessimist, cheery, planner, thorough, insightful, pious, proud

**Drives:** Eradicating the inhuman; great plans; narrow escapes

**Combat Stats:**
- Health Threshold: 4-7 | Armor: 2 | Health: 10
- Morale Threshold: 3 | Grit: 1 | Morale: 8
- Sway: 5 (Damage +1 from prayer)
- Warfare: 8 (Damage +1 from *Trial* battleaxe)

**Key Abilities:** Taunt 1, Trustworthy 1, Know Monstrosities 3, Leechcraft 1, Prophecy 1, Spot Frailty 2, Wilderness Mastery 1

**Gear:** Scarred chainmail, dented shield, *Trial* battleaxe, conspiracy theories (all true), deep faith, few human friends

**Design Notes:** Foyle combines cross-profession investigative abilities with monster-hunting expertise. He maintains friendships with certain monstrosities and serves as a bridge to Eversink's hidden inhuman demimonde.
