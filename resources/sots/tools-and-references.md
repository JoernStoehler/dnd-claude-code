# SotS Tools and References Index

## Online Tools (by Matthew Breen)

All at https://monstar.co.nz/matt/sotstools/

- **Hero Generator**: https://monstar.co.nz/matt/sotsgen/ — Build a character from scratch or randomly generate a starting template. Exports to PDF or short-form stat block.
- **Adversary Builder**: https://monstar.co.nz/matt/sotsgen/adversary — Generate stat blocks for adversaries and monstrosities.
- **Triskadane Deck**: https://monstar.co.nz/matt/sotsgen/triskadane — Deal cards to populate Eversink's secret ruling council. Determines political climate through faction affiliations.
- **Name Generators**: https://monstar.co.nz/matt/sotsgen/guilds — Names for people, professional societies, and thieves' guilds.
- **Plot Hook Generator**: https://monstar.co.nz/matt/sotsgen/hooks — Faction-weighted plot hook generation.
- **The Due Monete**: https://monstar.co.nz/matt/sotsgen/duemonete — Eversink cultural art form tool.
