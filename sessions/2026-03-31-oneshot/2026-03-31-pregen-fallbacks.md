# Pregen Fallback Characters — One-Shot 2026-03-31

For latecomers who need to sit down and play immediately. These are completed versions of the Warrior, Thief, and Sellsword templates. Hand one over, point at the name/adjectives/drives at the top, say "change those if you want, or just play as-is."

**Defense — Morale:** Morale Threshold 3, Grit 1, Morale 8

**Investigative Abilities:**
- Know Monstrosities 2
- Spot Frailty 1
- Tactics of Death 1
- Intimidation 1
- Trustworthy 1
- Vigilance 1
- *(2 points unspent — assign during play)*

**Allegiances:** Allies: Mercenaries 1, Commoners 1. Enemy: Monstrosities 1.

**Gear:** *Grundtvig* (hand-axe, chipped but sharp); studded leather jerkin (smells of campfire); a journal of creature sketches; a pouch of rock salt "just in case"; a scar across the left eye from something he won't talk about
