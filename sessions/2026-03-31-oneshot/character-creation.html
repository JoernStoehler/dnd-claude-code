Character Creation — Step by Step

Fill in the official character sheet as you go. Takes ~10 minutes. You can leave points unspent and assign during play.
1
Profession — pick one (or mix). This is a label, not a cage.
Sentinel (investigator, inquisitor) · Sorcerer (magic user) · Thief (rogue, spy) · Warrior (fighter, barbarian) · or mix freely.
Write it in the Profession field.
2
Adjectives — 3–5 words describing your personality. Quick reference for how you act under pressure.
Write in the Adjectives field.
3
Drives — "What is Best in Life?" Answer three things. These are your motivations. When a Drive is relevant, +1 to any roll.
Write in the three "What is Best in Life?" lines.
4
Investigative Abilities — fill bubbles on the sheet. Points by player count:
PlayersAbility points

Spend on any Investigative ability from any profession. You also get 3 additional points for Allegiances (Step 5).
Bonus: If ALL your profession picks (not Social, not Allegiances) come from one profession, +1 free point.
See the Ability Lookup sheet for what each ability does.
5
Allegiances — using your 3 additional Allegiance points, assign:
2 points to Allies (one faction or split between two) — people who help you
1 point to an Enemy — a faction that opposes you
Write faction names + fill bubbles in the Allegiances section.

6

Max rule: no ability can exceed 2× your next-highest ability.
Have at least one combat ability (Warfare, Sway, or Sorcery) at 5+. At 8+ you get a Talent — worth it.
7
Health & Morale — split 18 points between them (min 3 each). Circle your rank on the track.
10+ in either → Threshold becomes 4 (instead of 3). Write your Threshold in the box.
With 18 points, you can reach 10+ in one stat (e.g. 10/8) but not both.
8
Gear — write 5+ items that define who you are. One is your weapon (sets Damage Modifier: small +0, medium +1, large +2). One can be armor (leather = Armor 1, chain = Armor 2).
5+ items of gear → Grit 1. Write Grit 1 in the Grit box.
9
Sorcery — only if you have Corruption 1+. Check whether your Sorcery attacks Health or Morale (permanent choice). Pick 1 Sphere per Corruption rank. Write Spheres on the sheet. Pick your source (Serpentine writings or Demon/Spirit).
See the Sorcery Cheat Sheet for details.
10
Name — anything you like. Fantasy Italian-ish fits the setting, but not required. Skip True Name (not relevant for one-shot). Ignore Wealth/Lifestyle fields.
Stuck? Leave points unspent and assign during play when you realize you need something. You can also redistribute freely after your first session.
