Quick Stat Block — If They Need One

Background NPC (mook): Health 1, Morale 1, Threshold 3, no Armor/Grit. Warfare +0 Fixed Damage 3 / Sway +0 Fixed Damage 3. Malus 3. Refresh: 1.
Tougher variant: raise Health or Morale to 5-8, add Armor 1 or Grit 2, raise one attack to +1. Malus 8. Refresh: 3.
Rule of thumb: If you don't want to track their HP, they go down in one hit (mooks) or after a dramatic moment (named NPCs — you decide when).
