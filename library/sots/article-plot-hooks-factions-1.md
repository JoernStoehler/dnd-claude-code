# Serpentine Plot Hooks: Rebellious Factions Part 1

**By Kevin Kulp**

This article provides game masters with six faction-based plot hooks for *Swords of the Serpentine*, focused on rebellious characters who challenge the status quo. Each entry includes a concept, character description, and adventure hook.

## Ancient Nobility

**Character:** Gabrielle Ambari, a young noble rejecting her family's values and working against noble interests through unconventional methods and social activism.

**Hook:** "The heroes are hired by Gabrielle's family to help her, or she hires them to sabotage the nobility."

## Church of Denari

**Character:** Orthus Swanbeak, an elderly church official secretly working as a heretic to undermine official doctrine through institutional manipulation.

**Hook:** The party investigates church treachery while Orthus "assists," potentially misdirecting the investigation.

## City Watch

**Character:** Captain Settana Malfi encourages vigilantes rather than enforcing formal law, leveraging adventurers for problem-solving.

**Hook:** After arrest, Settana releases the party in exchange for handling quasi-legal issues.

## Commoners

**Character:** Pendle Shortstreet, a popular grocer organizing the populace against the government while resisting faction manipulation.

**Hook:** The party helps Pendle evade blackmail while navigating multiple factions seeking his support or elimination.

## Guild of Architects and Canal-Watchers

**Character:** Liera Oberi, a deformed master architect breaking her vows to resurface in the city with sorcerous abilities.

**Hook:** Factions compete to recruit or eliminate Liera before others gain her architectural secrets.

## Mercanti

**Character:** Alchemist Zorios Sandefar uses his wealth for philanthropic projects, threatening Mercanti profitability.

**Hook:** "5 Wealth per person" if the party protects Zorios from assassins, though enemies offer double to eliminate him.
