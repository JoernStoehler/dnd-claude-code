# Swords of the Serpentine as High Fantasy

**Source:** https://pelgranepress.com/2025/06/28/swords-of-the-serpentine-as-high-fantasy/
**Author:** Kevin Kulp

## High Concept

This guide adapts Swords of the Serpentine for traditional fantasy play, featuring non-human characters, classical magic systems, and divine miracles from patron deities.

## Character Generation

### Professions

The system provides expanded profession options mimicking class-based games:

- **Barbarian**: Uses Danger Sense, Spot Frailty, Tactics of Death, Wilderness Mastery with social abilities like Taunt and Intimidation
- **Bard**: Combines Arcana, City Secrets, Ridiculous Luck, and Scurrilous Rumors with heavy social ability investment
- **Cleric**: Features Divinity, Leechcraft, Prophecy, Spirit Sight aligned with deity interests
- **Druid**: Includes Divinity, Know Monstrosities, Vigilance, Wilderness Mastery with animal transformation capability
- **Fighter**: Employs Danger Sense, Know Monstrosities, Spot Frailty, Tactics of Death
- **Monk**: Combines Laws & Traditions, Spirit Sight, Spot Frailty, Vigilance
- **Paladin**: Merges Divinity, Laws & Traditions, Spirit Sight, Tactics of Death
- **Ranger**: Uses Know Monstrosities, Skulduggery, Vigilance, Wilderness Mastery
- **Thief**: Applies City Secrets, Ridiculous Luck, Scurrilous Rumors, Skulduggery
- **Wizard**: Features Arcana, Forgotten Lore, Leechcraft, Prophecy

### Non-Human Heroes

"Use the rules for non-human heroes to play elves, dwarves, and more." Additional ancestry rules are published regularly in See Page XX.

### Power Level

For extended campaigns, start characters at Fledgling tier with 8 Investigative ranks, 25 General ranks, split 15 Health/Morale points, two Allies, and one Enemy.

### Enhanced Allegiances

Develop detailed reasons why Allies support characters and why Enemies actively oppose them.

## Abilities

### Investigative Abilities

**Arcana** replaces Thaumaturgy as the primary magical investigation ability, granting access to all spheres for standard wizards without negative consequences, though less potent than Corruption-fueled alternatives.

**Danger Sense** (renamed from Felonious Intent) warns of impending traps and ambushes blocking adventure completion.

**Spirit Sight** now gains one sphere per rank: Arcane (detects Corruption and arcane magic), Dimensions (senses planar gates), Divine (identifies divine magic), and Spirits (perceives ghosts and nature spirits).

**Divinity** functions as a new investigative ability operating at Corruption's power level:

- Ranks grant clues about deities, religion, and divine magic
- "For every rank of Divinity, gain a sphere related to your god's area of belief"
- Spending pool points enables Miracles with similar potency to Corruption
- Miracles themed by divine spheres wrack either Health or Morale (player choice)
- Casting powerful Miracles temporarily reduces maximum ranks: "For every pool point of Divinity you spent on a miracle, temporarily lose that many ranks from your Health or Morale ability"
- Effects refresh at adventure start

**Wilderness Mastery** includes ocean Navigation; ranks indicate animal companion capability.

### General Abilities

**Sorcery removal**: Delete this ability; use Warfare (Health attacks) and Sway (Morale attacks) instead, described through Arcana or Divinity spheres.

**Bind Wounds** now auto-heals users as efficiently as others. The 8+ talent becomes "Quick, Drink This!" allowing one person healing per round without using actions.

**Burglary** 8+ talent transforms to "Lucky Grab," permitting players to select stolen loot (within reason) when using Burglary.

### Wealth

In cities, Wealth spends establish lifestyle and repute normally. In wilderness or travel, spending 1-5 points helps navigate dangers using a -2 to +2 scale where players spend positive points to mitigate hazards and GMs use negatives to worsen conditions.

## Other Rules Changes

- **Three Best Things in Life** allows rerolling one die instead of adding +1
- **Damage rolling** raises the lowest die result, not a specific die
- **Unconsciousness tests** are eliminated when Health or Morale drops below 0
- **Rare item acquisition** gates certain items behind Allegiance requirements
- **Refresh tokens** increase: 1 per Mook, 2 per Hero for named foes, 3 per Hero for major antagonists, 4 per Hero for memorable villains

## Design Philosophy

"Game rules are designed to match play to a particular play experience. They aren't an end in themselves." GMs are encouraged to modify rules to suit their campaign's tone without hesitation.
